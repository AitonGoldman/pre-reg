Instructions

This site will allow you to pay $20 for 10 tickets in the 2018 Intergalactic charity pinball tournament (The $20 is not refundable). You will be asked to enter the following things : Your name. Your email address. Your credit card info

To start this process, click the button at the bottom of the page. Once you have been registered then you will be given your player number and pin. This information will also be emailed to you. You will need this information in order to play in the tournament.

You will also need this information to login to the Papa Scoring Software which allows you to place yourself on queues and check results.

Note that the Intergalactic tournament is a limited HERB format - this means that you can not get more tickets once you have used your 10 tickets. More tournament details here.
